Crystal Collector!

There are four Rupees below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to our random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above our random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

Click a Rupee to add to your score!
