Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Rupees below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to our random number. You lose the game if your total score goes about our random number.

The calue of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
